Palkia

Water / Dragon Type

Palkia is a Legendary Pokémon known for its control over space.

Abilities

It can distort dimensions and create portals through space.

Lore

Palkia is said to live in a separate dimension. It is one of the creation trio alongside Dialga and Giratina. It represents space itself, making it one of the most powerful Legendary Pokémon in existence.
